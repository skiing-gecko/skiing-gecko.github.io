Protein Folding with AlphaFold: A Medical Breakthrough

The AI system AlphaFold, from DeepMind, predicts the 3D structure of a protein using only its amino acid sequence. Is this one of computing's biggest contributions to society in recent times?

Photo by Rulo Davila

Why Is It So Important?

There are many ways in which the prediction of a protein structure can be beneficial. Some examples are:

  • Fighting diseases
  • Developing new medicines
  • Studying the evolution of proteins
  • Developing enzymes to break down plastics
  • And many more...
